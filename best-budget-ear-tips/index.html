Best budget ear tips

Quick answer

For the best balance of price and performance, grab the Foam Masters Memory Foam Ear Tips, they're our top budget pick.

If you prioritize comfort above all, the EarBuddyz Memory Foam Tips are the editor’s choice; for maximum size options in one pack, the Diofit Memory Foam Tips are the most popular pick.

Replacing your AirPods’ stock silicone tips with memory foam can transform the listening experience. Foam tips conform to your ear canal, improving passive noise isolation, bass response, and overall fit security, especially during workouts or commutes. The catch is that not all budget foam tips are created equal: some shed material quickly, others lose their shape after a few days. When choosing, consider foam density (firmer foam holds shape longer but takes more effort to compress), sizing (multiple sizes help dial in the perfect seal), and nozzle compatibility, most AirPods Pro models use the same oval nozzle, but third-party tips vary in how snugly they click on. All three picks below work with AirPods Pro 1st and 2nd gen, and each has a slightly different trade-off between cost, comfort, and longevity.

Best for specific needs

Best for noise isolation

When passive noise reduction is your top priority, a denser foam that creates a full seal matters most. The Foam Masters Memory Foam Tips fit snugly and block out a significant amount of ambient sound, making them ideal for noisy commutes or open-plan offices. The trade-off is slightly less comfort for some ears, but the isolation payoff is real. Our pick: Foam Masters Memory Foam Ear Tips.

Best for all-day comfort

If you wear AirPods for hours at a stretch, during calls, podcasts, or music, softness and ease of insertion become critical. The EarBuddyz foam compresses effortlessly and expands gently, reducing ear fatigue. They also stay in place without excessive pressure, which is why they earned the editor’s pick for comfort. Our pick: EarBuddyz Memory Foam Ear Tips.

Best for versatility and sizing

Not every ear canal is the same, and having multiple sizes to test is a huge advantage. The Diofit pack includes four pairs across three sizes, letting you find the right seal without buying separate packages. The firmer foam also appeals to listeners who prioritize a secure, immobile fit over plush feel. Our pick: Diofit Memory Foam Ear Tips.

Our verdict

For most people on a tight budget, the Foam Masters Memory Foam Tips offer the best combination of price, sound improvement, and fit security. If you’re willing to spend a little more for noticeably better comfort and longevity, the EarBuddyz tips are worth the upgrade. And if you need to accommodate multiple ear shapes in one buy, the Diofit multi-pack is the practical choice. Whichever you choose, swapping to foam is one of the cheapest upgrades you can make to your AirPods experience.
