The best ear tips of 2026

Quick answer

The Comply SmartCore memory foam tips offer the best all-around fit and noise isolation for AirPods Pro.

The SpinFit CP1025 silicone tips are a premium choice for those who prefer a more breathable, stable feel, while Foam Masters deliver exceptional value on a budget.

The ear tips that come with your wireless earbuds are a compromise. They need to fit a range of ear shapes, which means they often don’t fit any one pair perfectly. A poor seal leads to weak bass, tinny sound, and the constant fear of a bud falling out. Swapping to aftermarket tips is the single most effective upgrade you can make. Your choice comes down to two materials: memory foam, which expands to fill your ear canal for a custom-like seal, or silicone, which is washable and more durable but can be slippery. Foam tips generally offer better noise isolation and bass response, while silicone is easier to maintain and better for sweaty activities. Consider how you use your earbuds most, what your ear canals are like, and whether you’re willing to replace tips every few months.
